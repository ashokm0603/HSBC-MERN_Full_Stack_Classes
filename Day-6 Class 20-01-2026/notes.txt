Animation properties : 
----------------------
    ->Animation are introduced in the version CSS3
    ->Animation are used to apply the stylings from one style to another style for particular duration 
    ->To Start the Animation we need to @keyframes
    ->Animation will work only when it contains animation-duration 


    1. animation-name 
    2. animation-duration
    3. animation-iteration-count : number , infinite
    4. animation-delay 
    5. animation-direction: normal , reverse, alternate , alternate-reverse 
    6. animation-timing-function: linear , ease , ease-in , ease-out, ease-in-out 
    

	Syntax: 

        @keyframe animationName{

        }
